Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The BU Alumni Portal ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, graduation year, and contact details provided during registration
  • Usage data such as pages visited and features used within the portal
  • Voluntary information submitted through forms, feedback, and event registrations

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your alumni membership and provide portal services
  • To send event notifications, newsletters, and relevant updates (you may opt out at any time)
  • To improve portal features and user experience
  • To process donations and membership payments securely

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except where required by law or with your explicit consent. Partner organisations listed in the Business Network section operate under their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at alumni@bu.edu to exercise these rights.

Cookies

This portal uses essential cookies to maintain your session. No third-party tracking cookies are used without your consent.
