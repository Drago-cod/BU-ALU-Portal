Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my membership tier later?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time by submitting a new membership form and paying the difference. Your benefits activate immediately upon confirmation.

How does the VVP insurance benefit work?

VVP members receive an Alumni Insurance Certificate upon payment confirmation. This certificate qualifies you for group insurance coverage through our partner provider. Details are sent to your registered email.

Are membership fees refundable?

Membership fees are non-refundable once processed. If you have concerns, please contact us at alumni@bu.edu before completing your payment.

What is a one-time donation?

A one-time donation goes directly to the BU Alumni Scholarship Fund. You do not receive membership benefits, but you will receive a donation receipt for your records.

How do I access Grads Links after registering?

Once your membership is verified (within 2 business days), you will receive login credentials by email. Grads Links and gated features will then be accessible from your member dashboard.
