Our Mission

Who We Are

BU Alumni Portal is a global network of Bugema University graduates dedicated to lifelong learning, professional growth, and community impact. We exist to keep the BU spirit alive long after graduation day.

Founded on the belief that a university's greatest asset is its people, we connect over 389,000 alumni across 72 chapters worldwide � creating opportunities, celebrating achievements, and giving back to the next generation.
